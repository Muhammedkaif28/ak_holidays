What is MEA Attestation?

MEA Attestation refers to the official process of validating documents by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India. It is a mandatory step for documents issued in India to be legally recognized both within the country and abroad. Depending on the destination country and purpose, the MEA either affixes an Attestation Stamp or issues an Apostille.

Since March 1, 2012, the MEA has outsourced the submission and collection process to authorized service providers. Individuals must submit original documents with photocopies and passport copies via these agencies.
